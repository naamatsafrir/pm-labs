## Rare-need placement pattern (for instructor validation)

- **Data Residency**: only in narrow APAC-heavy ranges of `nps-survey-responses`, `churn-exit-interviews`, and `cab-session-transcripts`.
- **Compliance Certification**: concentrated in late-range `churn-exit-interviews` plus limited `cab-session-transcripts`.
- **Offline Access**: only appears in late-range `support-tickets`, `feature-requests`, and `community-forum-posts`.
- **Multi-Language Support**: APAC-only signal in constrained ranges across `sales-call-notes`, `nps-survey-responses`, and `support-tickets`.
- **Version History and Rollback**: constrained to late-range `customer-interviews`, `internal-meeting-notes`, and some `cab-session-transcripts`.

## Interpretation notes

- Approximately 30% of generated files are noise-only (positive sentiment/off-topic/non-critical comments).
- The remaining corpus mixes direct pain statements and adjacent process friction.
- Good student outputs deduplicate synonyms and normalize wording while preserving evidence.
