# Product Overview

Worklane supports roadmap creation, prioritization, and cross-functional planning. Core users include product managers, PMO teams, engineering leaders, and executive stakeholders.

## Known Constraints
- Existing role model is too coarse in enterprise settings.
- Reporting depth varies by workspace maturity.
- Integration demands have increased as customers connect Worklane to adjacent systems.

## Important
Do not jump to solution design. The required output is a customer needs catalog focused on pain and unmet needs.
