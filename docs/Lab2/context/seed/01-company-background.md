# Worklane Company Background

Worklane is a B2B product planning platform used by multi-team organizations. Customers use Worklane for roadmap planning, intake management, and portfolio visibility.

## Current Situation
- Adoption has grown across enterprise and upper mid-market accounts.
- Expansion opportunities are high, but enterprise conversion depends on governance and controls.
- Feedback was gathered from interviews, support tickets, sales calls, NPS responses, community posts, QBRs, churn exits, and CAB sessions over the last 12 months.
