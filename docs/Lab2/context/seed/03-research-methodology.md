# Research Methodology and Rules

## Required Outcome
Produce one Customer Needs Document with:
1. Distinct customer needs (deduplicated).
2. Relative frequency and supporting evidence.
3. Affected segments and business impact.

## Working Rules
- Context is too large for one-shot processing.
- You must process artifacts in chunks and maintain a local summary file after each chunk.
- Final synthesis must be based on those chunk summaries.
- Missing files or ranges will lead to incomplete results.

## Evaluation
Your output will be compared to a reference set of expected needs. You are not expected to match wording exactly, but missing needs and incorrect frequency direction are quality gaps.
